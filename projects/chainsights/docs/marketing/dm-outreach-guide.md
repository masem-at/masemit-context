## Who to Target (Finding the Right People)

### âœ… Good Targets:

1. **Top Delegates** (High voting power, active participation)
   - Find: Snapshot space â†’ Delegates tab â†’ Sort by voting power
   - Must have: Recent activity (voted in last 30 days)

2. **Core Team Members** (Engaged in DAO operations)
   - Find: Twitter, Discord, DAO documentation
   - Look for: Official roles, regular governance posts

3. **Active Commenters** (People who comment on proposals)
   - Find: Snapshot proposal discussions, forum threads
   - Shows: They care about governance details

### âŒ Bad Targets:

- Random community members (not governance-focused)
- Inactive accounts (haven't voted in 3+ months)

- People you've never engaged with on social media

## Pre-DM Checklist

Before sending ANY cold DM:

- [ ] Confirmed they're active in this DAO's governance (voted recently)
- [ ] Found 1-2 specific findings they'd care about
- [ ] Have Twitter/social profile to verify they're real/active
- [ ] Engaged with 1-2 of their recent posts (like/retweet)
- [ ] NOT sending to multiple people from same DAO simultaneously
- [ ] Have the report ready to send if they say yes

**If all boxes checked â†’ Send DM**

**If any box unchecked â†’ Wait or reconsider**

## When to Choose Forum vs. DM

| Scenario | Approach | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| Active forum (10+ posts/week) | **Forum post** | Better ROI, builds public credibility |
| Dead forum (<5 posts/week) | **DM to delegates** | No one will see forum post |
| No forum, only Snapshot | **DM to delegates** | Only direct channel available |
| Both active forum + Snapshot | **Forum first**, DM later if needed | Public visibility > private outreach |

**Default Rule:** Forum first, DM only when necessary.
