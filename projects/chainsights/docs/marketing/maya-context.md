## Current Strategy (PIVOT - DEC 19, 2024)

### The New Approach: Forum-Based Value Delivery

**Core Principle:** Go where customers are. Be valuable first. Sell later.

**Target:** Mid-size struggling DAOs (NOT the big names)
- Snapshot spaces with <10k members
- Declining participation rates
- New DAOs (launched in last 6 months)
- Active forum discussions

**Channels:**
1. DAO-specific Discourse forums (Optimism, Arbitrum, Uniswap, Gitcoin, etc.)
2. Commonwealth.im (one account, multiple DAOs)
3. NOT Snapshot (it's voting platform, not discussion forum)

**Tactics:**
1. Generate free reports for 5-10 target DAOs
2. Post analysis in THEIR forums (no pitch, just value)
3. Respond to governance discussions with data insights
4. Post 3x/week for 6 weeks minimum
5. Build credibility before asking for anything

**Timeline:**
- Week 1-2: Post in 3 forums, establish presence
- Week 3-4: People start noticing
- Week 5-6: First real conversations
- Week 7-8: First customer (maybe)
