## Tone Guidelines

### Voice
- **Confident** - We know what we're talking about
- **Direct** - No corporate fluff
- **Slightly provocative** - Challenge assumptions
- **Helpful** - Want DAOs to improve

### Do
- Use data and specifics
- Challenge "decentralization theater"
- Engage genuinely with governance discussions
- Share insights freely (builds authority)

### Don't
- Sound like a generic crypto shill
- Use excessive emojis
- Hype or pump language
- Engage in drama/controversy
