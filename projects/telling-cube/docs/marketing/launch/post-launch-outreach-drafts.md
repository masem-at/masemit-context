## 3. DM Personalization Tips

**Before sending ANY DM:**
1. Check their recent posts - mention something specific
2. Check their headline - tailor the pain point
3. Check if they're IBCS certified (often in headline or about section)
4. Check their company - B2B consulting? Corporate? Training?

**First line examples:**
- "Saw your post about [X] - really resonated with me..."
- "Your work on [project/company] caught my attention..."
- "Fellow [Austrian/controller/consultant] here..."

**What NOT to do:**
- Generic copy-paste with no personalization
- Immediate pitch without question
- Long paragraphs (keep it scannable)
- Multiple follow-ups in 24h

## 7. LinkedIn Search Strategy (Sales Navigator)

**Sales Navigator filters to use:**
1. **Keywords:** `IBCS` or `"IBCS certified"` or `business reporting`
2. **Title:** Trainer, Consultant, Controller, BI Developer
3. **Geography:** DACH region first (Germany, Austria, Switzerland)
4. **Posted on LinkedIn:** Last 30 days (CRITICAL - shows active users)
5. **Industry:** Management Consulting, IT Services, Financial Services

**Basic LinkedIn search queries (if no Sales Nav):**
- `"IBCS certified"` (in quotes)
- `"IBCS consultant"`
- `"business reporting" trainer`
- `"financial reporting" workshop`
- `IBCS` + filter by "Posts" (find engaged commenters)

**Profile signals to look for:**
- "IBCS" in headline or certifications
- "Trainer" or "Consultant" in title
- Posts about reporting, dashboards, data visualization
- Works at consulting firm or training company
- Posted recently (active = responsive)

**Why "Posted on LinkedIn" filter matters:**
- 80% of LinkedIn users are passive (rarely check)
- Recent posters are 5-10x more likely to respond
- You can reference their post = instant personalization

**Outreach workflow:**
1. Use Sales Nav to find 20 prospects with "Posted in last 30 days"
2. Check their recent post - note the topic
3. Send connection request with Template B (reference their post)
4. Once accepted (24-72h), send full DM from templates
5. Follow up after 48-72h if no response
