### Feature Gaps Identified

- More years, budgets, forecasts, multi-year plans
- Hierarchies in structural dimensions
- Additional domains (HR, ESG)
- IBCS visualization refinement

### Market Skepticism

Jürgen questioned commercial viability outside open source because:
1. IBCS trainers use standardized curricula (don't need custom data models)
2. Workshop+ is the only training with potential use case
3. Templates need consistent data models - but IBCS already built one

---

## Critical Insight Discovered

> "Ohne es allerdings zu nutzen, weil uns die Datenanbindung in unserer virtuellen Organisation zu kompliziert ist."

**Translation:** The IBCS Institute built their own data model but CAN'T USE IT because data connection is too complex.

**Strategic Implication:** Data connectivity is an unsolved pain point even for the creators of IBCS standards.

---

## Team Analysis & Conclusions

### Market Positioning Pivot

| Old Positioning | New Positioning |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| "Generate enterprise data models" | "IBCS-compliant reporting made effortless" |
| Target: IBCS trainers | Target: IBCS practitioners post-certification |

### Target Audience Refinement

Jürgen thinks like an **institute operator**, not a buyer. Our actual market:
- CFOs preparing board decks
- BI analysts in enterprises with IBCS standards
- Consultants delivering client reports
- 10,000+ IBCS certification alumni who struggle to implement

### Value Proposition Clarity

The model generation is table stakes. The real value:
1. **Instant IBCS visualization compliance**
2. **Zero-config data connection** (solving the pain IBCS Institute couldn't)
3. **Enterprise-ready without enterprise complexity**
