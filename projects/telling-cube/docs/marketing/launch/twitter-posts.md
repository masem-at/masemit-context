## Platform Notes

**Twitter/X is different from LinkedIn:**

- Threads: 5-8 tweets optimal (more = drop-off)

- Hashtags: 1-2 max (or none - they're less important now)
- Video: Upload natively (don't just paste YouTube link)
- Best times: 8-10 AM or 5-7 PM (your audience's timezone)

## Video Upload Instructions

**For native Twitter video (recommended over YouTube link):**

1. Export video at 1080p (1920x1080)
2. Format: MP4, H.264 codec
3. Max length: 2:20 (your video is ~1:50, perfect)
4. Max file size: 512MB
5. Upload directly to Twitter (don't paste YouTube link)

**Why native matters:** Twitter suppresses external links. Native video gets 10x more reach.

## Hashtag Strategy

**Less is more on Twitter. Pick 0-2:**

- #buildinpublic (if you want indie hacker audience)
- #saas (broad B2B)
- None (often performs better - feels less promotional)

**Don't use:** Long hashtag chains like LinkedIn. Looks spammy on Twitter.
